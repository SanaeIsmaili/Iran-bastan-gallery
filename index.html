About Us

At Iran Bastan Gallery, we celebrate Iran’s artistic legacy through a curated selection of handmade and antique crafts. Our collection includes chandeliers, textiles, tablecloths, trays, vases, and many more unique items that blend beauty with tradition.

Each piece is carefully selected to bring timeless elegance and cultural richness into your home.
